COVID-19 U.S. States Growth

States:    Plus the states with the most cases

The y-axis uses a log scale, meaning that a straight line indicates exponential growth. The dotted lines are guides for different rates of daily exponential growth, 100%, 41%, 26%, 19%, i.e., doubling every 1 to 4 days.

In the table, the daily exponential growth percentage is determined by linear regression in the log domain with the 3 previous days.
